HypeOS
HypeOS is a lightweight operating system for old or less powerful PC's meant to perform basic tasks like web browsing, text editing, and playing media.

HypeOS Touch
HypeOS Touch is our second lightweight operating system designed for x86-based tablets and is meant to be touch-friendly.
