We are HyperActive. Our goal is to make good and high-quality software and operating systems for less powerful devices like phones, tablets, and minicomputers.

Right now we are developing HypeOS and HypeOS Touch. HypeOS is a very lightweight operating system for x86-based PC's. HypeOS Touch on the other hand, is an operating system designed for tablets and phones.
